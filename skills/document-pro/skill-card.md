## Description: <br>
Document Pro helps an agent read, parse, extract, convert, and summarize common document formats such as PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, TXT, and Markdown. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[Jackeven02](https://clawhub.ai/user/Jackeven02) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and developers use this skill when they need an agent to inspect user-provided documents, extract text or tables, answer questions about document contents, summarize reports, or convert supported formats. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intended to read and summarize documents supplied or explicitly identified by the user, which may expose confidential, regulated, or secret content to agent processing. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only with documents the user is comfortable having processed by the agent, and avoid confidential or regulated material unless the deployment context permits that processing. <br>
Risk: Scanned PDFs, complex formatting, images, and charts may be incomplete or misunderstood during extraction. <br>
Mitigation: Review extracted content against the original document and use OCR or specialized review for scanned or visually complex files. <br>
